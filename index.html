Mascot

Since the beginning of this franchise, the mascot has been Pikachu. Although pikachu was not a starter option in the original game, the anime had the main character, Ash, start out with pikachu. Pikachu has been by Ash's side throughout his entire journey in the anime. Pikachu not being as aggressive looking as some pokemon and a main staple in the franchise, makes Pikachu very marketable as well, one of the many reasons Pikachu was chosen to be the mascot of the franchise.
